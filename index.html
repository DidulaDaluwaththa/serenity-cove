About Us

Located in the heart of Ella, Sri Lanka, our restaurant offers a truly unique dining experience set within a breathtaking waterfall cave. Blending nature-inspired design with modern luxury, we create an immersive atmosphere where cascading waterfalls, ambient lighting, and carefully crafted spaces transport guests into a hidden world. We specialize in Sri Lankan fusion cuisine, using fresh, locally sourced ingredients to deliver authentic flavors with a contemporary twist. Guided by a strong zero-waste philosophy, we focus on sustainability while celebrating creativity in every dish. More than just a restaurant, we are a destination—dedicated to creating unforgettable moments through exceptional service, innovative design, and a deep connection to nature.

Arrive by water or land

Journey to our sanctuary via a scenic boat ride through cascading waters, or take the winding path that reveals the cave entrance. Each arrival promises a moment of wonder.
